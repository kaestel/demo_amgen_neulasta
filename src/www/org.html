Easy to Manage

Easy to Manage

Neulasta allows me to have the full protection with only one injection per chemotherapy cycle

No need to return to the clinic multiple times or self administer multiple injections through ideal dosing (with the self regulating mechanism)

Decreased burden on myself or my family as 1 injection provides optimal protection
Optimal dosing through self regulating mechanism avoids inadequate dosing and wasteful dosing

Optimal protection and support of chemotherapy with one injection per cycle

Optimal G-CSF support allow for improved chemotherapy delivery and therefore, improved RDI

Better RDI will lead to better treatment outcomes (including survival)

No need for multiple injections leaving more time to spend on other responsibilities

No need to emphasize the importance of continuing the complete number of injections as one shots provides optimal protection

through decreased incidence of FN and dose delays there is less need to reschedule patients in an already busy CT ward
